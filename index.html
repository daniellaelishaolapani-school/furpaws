ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a community-driven initiative under the local municipality of Trece Martires City dedicated to promoting responsible pet ownership and ensuring the safety and well-being of animals in our city.

HOW WE WORK

Our organization serves as an official animal registry, where pets are registered to help provide access to essential services, such as health monitoring, identification, and protection. By maintaining an updated database of pets, we support faster response during emergencies, prevent loss or theft, and contribute to public safety.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the benefits of registering my pet?

Registering your pet offers more advantages than you may think.


  • Easy Identification and Recovery
  • Proof of Ownership
  • Access to Health Monitoring
  • Increased Safety and Security
  • Faster Emergency Response
  • Supports Community Welfare

Why do I need to register my pet?

Registering your pet ensures their safety, supports their health, and helps build a more responsible and pet-friendly community.

What happens if I do not register my pet?

Failing to register your pet may lead to several challenges that can affect both you and your pets(s):


  • Difficulty In Finding Lost Pets
  • Lack of Proof of Ownership
  • Missed Health Monitoring
  • Slower Emergency Response
  • Non-Compliance With Local Policies

What information do I need to register my pet?

You will need basic details, such as owner and pet information.

Can I update any information I submit in the system?

Yes, you can update your user and pet(s)' information anytime under the "My Pets" and "Edit Profile" page in the User Dashboard.

How much would it be to register my pet?

Registration is completely free of charge!
